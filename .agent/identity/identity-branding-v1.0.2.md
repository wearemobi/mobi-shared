# M.O.B.I.™ · Identity & Branding Master Book v1.0.2

## 1. Technical Definition of the Logo

The **M.O.B.I.** logo is a minimalist engineering mark designed for high legibility and scalability.
* **Structure:** A perfect square, borderless design.
* **Grid Layout:** The interior space is divided into a 2x2 typographic grid by internal crosshairs/lines, containing the acronym:
  > **M.O.**
  > **B.I.**
* **Proportions:** The characters must be centered both horizontally and vertically within the square, occupying approximately 80% of the internal area.
* **Variants:**
    * **Negative:** White text and internal grid lines on a black or dark background (borderless).
    * **Monochrome:** Strict black and white usage; no gradients, drop shadows, or decorative elements are permitted.
* **Master Format:** All master assets must be maintained in **SVG** (Scalable Vector Graphics) to ensure sustainability (**SSD**) and infinite scalability.

## 3. Color Palette

The palette reflects an "Engineering-First" mindset, prioritizing high contrast and digital efficiency.
* **Primary (Engineering Black):** HEX `#000000` | RGB `0,0,0` | CMYK `75,68,67,90`
* **Secondary (Contrast White):** HEX `#FFFFFF` | RGB `255,255,255` | CMYK `0,0,0,0`

## 4. Typography Guide

* **Primary Font:** A geometric *sans-serif* (e.g., Roboto Mono or Montserrat) selected for all UI headings and corporate communication.
* **Technical Font:** A clean *monospace* font reserved strictly for code blocks, IDE interfaces, and the **M.O.B.I.** wordmark when used in plain text.

## 4. Brand Application Ecosystem

The **M.O.B.I.** ecosystem is divided into two specialized technical divisions. The prefix **"Mobi"** must be attached to the division name without spaces or hyphens.
* **MobiAI:** Focused on Artificial Intelligence Product Lines, Agentic research, and LLM orchestration.
* **MobiWare:** Focused on software engineering, SSD (Software Sustainability Design), DevOps, and Consultancy services.
